### Overall Assessment

**1. Work environment**  
The work environment is friendly, open, and comfortable. Everyone at FCAJ is always willing to help when I encounter difficulties, which made it easy for me to integrate and work effectively. Organizing regular social events or team-building activities would further strengthen team cohesion.

**2. Mentor / team admin support**  
My mentor provided thorough guidance and clear explanations whenever I needed help and encouraged me to try solving problems independently first. The team admin also supported administrative procedures and documentation, making the internship process smooth.

**3. Relevance between work and academic background**  
Assigned tasks aligned with what I studied and exposed me to practical topics. This helped reinforce my technical foundations while expanding practical knowledge and skills useful for future work.

**4. Learning opportunities & skill development**  
The internship offered many chances to develop both technical knowledge and soft skills. I became familiar with task management tools, improved teamwork and professional communication, and learned valuable practical experience from my mentor.

**5. Culture & team spirit**  
The company culture is positive, professional, and respectful. Team members readily collaborate and support each other, especially during high-pressure periods. This made me feel welcomed and part of the team.

**6. Intern policies / benefits**  
The company provides an internship allowance and accommodates scheduling needs when necessary. In addition, internal training sessions offered further learning opportunities to enhance knowledge and skills during the internship.
